Frequently Asked Questions

How is Profit/Loss calculated?

It is calculated by determining the current value of your holdings based on the selling price, subtracting the initial investment and any fees.

How do I use this tool?

Enter your total investment amount, the price you bought the coin at, and the price you sold (or plan to sell) at. Click calculate to see the results.

What is ROI?

ROI stands for Return on Investment. It is a percentage that shows how much profit or loss you have made relative to your initial investment.

Do fees matter?

Yes, trading fees (exchange fees, gas fees) can significantly impact your net profit, especially for frequent traders.

Does this work for any crypto?

Yes, the math is the same regardless of whether you are trading Bitcoin, Ethereum, or any other cryptocurrency.
