Frequently Asked Questions

What is a URL slug?

A URL slug is the part of a web address that identifies a particular page in a form readable by both users and search engines. For example, in example.com/this-is-a-slug, this-is-a-slug is the slug.

How does this tool work?

Type or paste your text, click "Generate Slug", and the tool will convert it to a lowercase, hyphen-separated, URL-safe string.

Why use slugs?

Slugs make URLs more readable, SEO-friendly, and easier to share.

Can I copy the slug?

Yes! Click the "Copy" button next to the generated slug to copy it to your clipboard.

Are there any restrictions?

Slugs only contain lowercase letters, numbers, and hyphens. Special characters and extra spaces are removed automatically.
