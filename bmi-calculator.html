Frequently Asked Questions

What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women.

How is BMI calculated?

BMI is calculated by dividing your weight (in kilograms) by your height (in meters) squared.

What are the BMI categories?

- Underweight: Less than 18.5
- Normal weight: 18.5 to 24.9
- Overweight: 25 to 29.9
- Obese: 30 or greater

Is BMI accurate for everyone?

BMI is not a perfect measure as it doesn't account for factors like muscle mass, bone density, age, and sex.
